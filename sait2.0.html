FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions)

Q: How to get roles? , A: Getting roles is easy. You can complete tasks to get them, win them in Giveaways or buy with Star-ries! .

Q: Can you buy roles with Robux? , A: Yes, you can. Such roles as Premium which require Star-ries, can be bought with Robux! But some roles like Moderator require an application .

Q: Where to apply for roles? , A: As you know, roles like Moderator require an application. You can apply by sending me any direct message on Discord. I'll give you some questions, give you a challenge and then tell you if you deserve the role or not.
